About Me I am a registered and licensed Planner and Lead EIA/EA Expert; as well as a qualified and experienced GIS Specialist in private practice in Kenya. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Planning and a Master of Science degree in GIS from the University of Nairobi and currently serving as the Technical Director at GIS Limited, based in Nairobi, Kenya.I’m an active Corporate Member of the Kenya Institute of Planners (KIP) where I am the current Honorary Chairperson. I’m also a Corporate Member of the Commonwealth Association of Planners (CAP); a Lead Expert Member of the Environment Institute of Kenya (EIK); and a Patron of the Kenya Young Planners (KYP).
